about me

I design digital stuff

currently at Urban as a product designer

I enjoy design problems that are neublous and that require creative solutions and am a self-starter (I coded this site by hand). My experience as a deaf person also informs my design methodology from an unique perspective.

Outside of work and design, I like climbing rocks and reading! I was raised in Hong Kong and Sydney and undertook my BA (Hons) Design at Goldsmiths College, University of London where I achieved First Class Honors.

I enjoy working in both teams and individually, and am always eager for opportunities- shoot me an email, or have a look at my cv.
