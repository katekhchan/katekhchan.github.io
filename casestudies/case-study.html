Case study title

ui/ux design, performance marketing

Project specs

As Urban evolved in both brand positioning (moving away from massage-only services) and tone of voice, we found that the user experience was becoming increasingly disjointed, especially with the colours. Initial outcomes were to present a data-driven palette that encapsulated what Urbanâ€™s new ToV was, while fulfilling differing needs from the marketing and product departments

Duration

Late Feb 2021 - Present

Role

Led and planned workshops, collaborated with designers and developers to come up with a visual data-backed solution that blends brand identity, accessibility, product & marketing needs.

Executed user testing in tandem with the project manager and kept everyone accountable for thier work.
